The Mission

Why We Do It

We have set a mission to provide supplies to places that are unable to get care packages due to rough terrains. We decided to work with drones to Deliver these packages to help the people in need in a timeley manner. There at two types of drones that are available for use, a long range drone and a short range drone. The care packages range from blood donations and emergency kits containing food, water and medicines for any situation. Our drone deliveries are perfect for people that live in remote areas and for deliveries in disaster areas.
